    Our History

    The story of Kids Transportation Service began when the CEO and his wife had their first son and faced the challenge of balancing their demanding jobs with ensuring a safe and reliable transportation service for him and other children. They recognized that many busy parents shared the same predicament. Motivated by their own experiences, they launched Kids Transportation Service in 2020.

    Kids Transportation Service embraces technology by providing parents with user-friendly mobile apps. These apps offer real-time updates on their children's whereabouts, giving parents peace of mind. Serving over 15 schools and connecting with hundreds of parents, Kids Transportation Service prioritizes child safety and convenience through the power of technology.

    Gone are the days when you had to entrust your child's safety to a stranger when it comes to school transportation. We provide you with the information you need to know about your child's drivers, and our student profile feature allows your driver to be familiar with your kids.
